The Four Tones — 四声
Tone is everything in Mandarin. The same syllable "ma" has four completely different meanings depending on tone. Master these before everything else.
Tone 1 — First Tone
ˉ妈mā
High, level, sustained. Like a sustained musical note at the top.
Tone 2 — Second Tone
ˊ麻má
Rising. Like asking "What?" in English with surprise.
Tone 3 — Third Tone
ˇ马mǎ
Dipping low then rising. Like a surprised "Oh?" that goes down then up.
Tone 4 — Fourth Tone
ˋ骂mà
Sharp, short, falling. Like saying "Stop!" or "No!" firmly.
Neutral — Light Tone
·吗ma
Short, light, unstressed. No tone mark. Used for particles and suffixes.
Tone sandhi rule: Two 3rd tones together — the first changes to 2nd tone. 你好 (nǐhǎo → níhǎo). 也好 (yěhǎo → yéhǎo). The written pinyin stays nǐ but you say ní.
不 (bù) changes: Before a 4th tone, 不 becomes bú. 不是 (bú shì), 不要 (bú yào). Before 1st/2nd/3rd tones, it stays bù.
一 (yī) changes: Before 4th tone → yí. Before 1st/2nd/3rd → yì. Alone or last → yī.

Core Grammar — 核心语法
是 (shì) — To Be
Structure: Subject + 是 + Noun 我是学生。Wǒ shì xuéshēng. — I am a student. 他是老师。Tā shì lǎoshī. — He is a teacher. Negative: 不是 (bú shì) 我不是中国人。Wǒ bú shì zhōngguórén. — I'm not Chinese.
有 (yǒu) — To Have | 没有 (méiyǒu) — Don't Have
我有一本书。Wǒ yǒu yī běn shū. — I have a book. 我没有钱。Wǒ méiyǒu qián. — I don't have money. WARNING: Never say 不有. Always use 没有 to negate 有.
Question Particle 吗 (ma) & Question Words
Add 吗 to end of statement → yes/no question: 你是学生吗？Nǐ shì xuéshēng ma? — Are you a student? 什么 (shénme) = what | 哪里/哪 (nǎlǐ/nǎ) = where 谁 (shéi) = who | 几 (jǐ) = how many (small) | 多少 (duōshǎo) = how much/many

Grammar — 语法
了 (le) — Completed Action
我吃了饭。Wǒ chī le fàn. — I (have) eaten. 他来了。Tā lái le. — He came / He's here now. 了 marks COMPLETION, not past tense. Time words + 了 = change of state.
想 / 要 / 会 / 能 — Modal Verbs
想 (xiǎng) = want to: 我想吃饭。I want to eat. 要 (yào) = need to / going to: 我要去了。I need to go now. 会 (huì) = know how to: 我会说中文。I can speak Chinese (learned skill). 能 (néng) = able to: 我能来。I can come (physically possible).
Measure Words — 量词 (essential!)
个 (gè) — general: 一个人 one person, 两个苹果 two apples 本 (běn) — books: 三本书 three books 张 (zhāng) — flat: 一张纸 one sheet of paper, 一张桌子 one table 杯 (bēi) — cups: 一杯水 one cup of water 条 (tiáo) — long/thin: 一条鱼 one fish, 一条路 one road 把 (bǎ) — hand-held: 一把椅子 one chair, 一把刀 one knife

Grammar — 语法
比 (bǐ) — Comparison
A + 比 + B + Adj [+ specific degree] 今天比昨天热。Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān rè. — Today is hotter than yesterday. 他比我高五厘米。Tā bǐ wǒ gāo wǔ límǐ. — He is 5cm taller than me. Not as adj: A + 没有 + B + Adj 今天没有昨天热。Today is not as hot as yesterday.
过 (guò) — Experience Marker
我去过中国。Wǒ qù guò zhōngguó. — I have been to China (in my life). 你吃过北京烤鸭吗？— Have you ever eaten Peking Duck? 没去过 = have never been; Compare 了 (completed) vs 过 (experienced)
因为...所以... & 虽然...但是...
因为 (yīnwèi) + reason, 所以 (suǒyǐ) + result: 因为下雨，所以我没去。Because it rained, I didn't go. 虽然 (suīrán) + concession, 但是 (dànshì) + main point: 虽然很贵，但是很好看。Although expensive, it's very beautiful.

Grammar — 语法
把 (bǎ) — Disposal Construction
Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Result/Direction/Complement 把书放在桌子上。Bǎ shū fàng zài zhuōzi shàng. — Put the book on the table. 我把作业做完了。Wǒ bǎ zuòyè zuò wán le. — I finished the homework. 他把手机丢了。Tā bǎ shǒujī diū le. — He lost his phone. Key: Use 把 when a specific object is disposed of / changed in location or state.
被 (bèi) — Passive Voice
Object + 被 + Agent + Verb + Result 书被他拿走了。Shū bèi tā ná zǒu le. — The book was taken by him. 我被老师批评了。Wǒ bèi lǎoshī pīpíng le. — I was criticized by the teacher. Note: 被 often implies something UNDESIRABLE happened to the subject.
越来越 / 越...越... — More and More / The more...the more
越来越 + Adj/V: getting more and more ___ 天气越来越热。The weather is getting hotter and hotter. 越 + V/Adj + 越 + V/Adj: the more...the more... 越学越有意思。The more you study, the more interesting it gets. 越吃越胖。The more you eat, the fatter you get.

Grammar — 语法
尽管...还是/仍然... — Even Though...Still...
尽管很累，他还是坚持工作。 Even though very tired, he still kept working. 尽管困难重重，我们仍然成功了。 Despite great difficulties, we still succeeded.
既然...就... — Since...Then...
既然你来了，就留下来吃饭吧。 Since you're here, just stay and eat. 既然决定了，就不要后悔。 Since you've decided, don't regret it.
无论/不管...都/也... — No Matter...Still...
无论天气怎样，我都去跑步。 No matter the weather, I still go running. 不管多难，他也不放弃。 No matter how hard, he still doesn't give up.

文言文 Classical Patterns
之 (zhī) = 的 in classical: 人之初，性本善。(Three Character Classic) 乎 (hū) = question/exclamation: 不亦乐乎？Is it not a joy? 也 (yě) = affirmation: 学而不思则罔，思而不学则殆。 To study without thinking is futile; to think without studying is dangerous. (Confucius) 者 (zhě) = one who: 知之者不如好之者。Those who know ≠ those who love it.

Traditional Characters & Taiwan Chinese
TOCFL uses Traditional Characters (繁體字) as used in Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau. The spoken Mandarin is identical to mainland — only the written form differs. This section covers Taiwan-specific vocabulary, Traditional characters, and TOCFL exam focus areas.
TOCFL A1–C2Traditional 繁體
Why TOCFL? — 為什麼考華測？
TOCFL vs HSK — Key Differences
TOCFL (Test of Chinese as a Foreign Language) is administered by Taiwan's NACCL. HSK is mainland China's exam. Both test standard Mandarin (普通話 / 普通話). TOCFL: Traditional Characters (繁體字) · Taiwan cultural context · 6 levels (A1→C2) HSK: Simplified Characters (简体字) · Mainland context · 6 levels (HSK 1→6) Pronunciation & grammar: IDENTICAL. Only written form and some vocabulary differs. TOCFL is required for: Taiwan university admission, APRC residence, Taiwan scholarships.

TOCFL Grammar Focus — 語法重點
TOCFL A1-A2: 把 sentence, 是...的 emphasis, reduplication
是...的 — emphasise when/where/how something happened: 他是昨天來的。(Tā shì zuótiān lái de.) — It was yesterday that he came. 我是在台灣學中文的。— It was in Taiwan that I studied Chinese. Reduplication — makes adjectives softer/cuter: 高高的 (gāogāo de) — nice and tall 慢慢地 (mànmàn de) — slowly, take it easy 好好學習 (hǎohǎo xuéxí) — study properly
TOCFL B1-B2: 連...都/也, 除了...之外, 不但...而且
連...都/也 (lián...dōu/yě) — even: 連小孩都知道。(Even children know this.) 連他也來了。(Even he came.) 除了...之外 (chúle...zhīwài) — apart from / besides: 除了中文之外，她還會日文。(Besides Chinese, she also knows Japanese.) 不但...而且 (búdàn...érqiě) — not only...but also: 她不但漂亮，而且聰明。(She is not only beautiful but also smart.)
TOCFL C1-C2: 與其...不如, 儘管...還是, Literary 文言 elements
與其...不如 (yǔqí...bùrú) — rather than...better to: 與其抱怨，不如行動。(Rather than complaining, better to act.) 儘管...還是 (jǐnguǎn...háishi) — even though...still: 儘管很累，他還是堅持完成了。(Even though exhausted, he still persisted to finish.) 台灣書面語 Taiwan formal writing often uses 之、其、於、乃、而 from Classical Chinese: 基於此，吾人應積極面對挑戰。(Based on this, we should actively face challenges.)
